Government Polytechnic for Women Kadapa established in 1985 is one of the premier diploma institutes for Women in Rayalaseema in 7.65 acres of land on the highway of Kadapa-Tirupati located at a distance of 4kms from Kadapa railway station and APSRTC bus station. The Institute is well known for its state-of-the art infrastructure conducive to a healthy academic environment and dedicated & qualified staff focusing on the wholesome development of the students. The institute is fully funded by Government of Andhra Pradesh as per allocation of the annual budget. It is also supported by AICTE and MHRD funds.
